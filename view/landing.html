Introducing iPhone 16 Pro

Design & Display

Crafted with Precision. Designed for the Future. The iPhone 16 Pro introduces a stunning aerospace-grade titanium frame, making it both incredibly strong and remarkably light. Its edge-to-edge ProMotion OLED display delivers ultra-smooth visuals at up to 120Hz, with brightness and color accuracy that adapts seamlessly to any lighting condition. The front and back are protected by Ceramic Shield, tougher than any smartphone glass, ensuring durability without compromise.

Performance & Battery

Power You Can Feel. Efficiency You Can Trust. At the heart of iPhone 16 Pro is the all-new A18 Bionic chip, engineered with advanced neural processing and extreme energy efficiency. Whether you're gaming, multitasking, or editing 4K videos, it handles everything effortlessly. And with a smarter battery management system, you get up to 2 days of battery life with fast-charging and improved wireless performance—so you stay powered longer, wherever you go.

Camera & Spatial Video

Redefining Mobile Photography and Immersion. The iPhone 16 Pro features a pro-grade triple-lens system with a 48MP main sensor, an ultra-wide lens, and a new periscope-style telephoto lens offering up to 10x optical zoom. Capture crisp details in low light with Night Mode, and shoot spatial videos that make your memories come alive on Apple Vision Pro. It’s not just a camera—it’s a cinematic powerhouse in your pocket.
